Continental Celtic goddess Alauna was a river divinity discovered in Wales, specifically in the river Alun located in Pembrokeshire, and in Brittany, specifically in the river Alaunus. It is also believed that she may have existed in Englandâ€™s Alun, a tributary of the Dee. Folklore tells that fish from this river would come out of the water just to stare at human men and would wink suggestively at women.
